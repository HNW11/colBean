About Us

We are a family run animal rescue and coffee shop in one. You can come in and order coffee and pet some of our free range dogs. Or you can meet one of our up for adoption animals and see if they are the perfect fit to your family. Either way all proceeds from the coffee shop, store and rescue go right back in to taking care of the animals we house.

Our Goal

To unite pets with their furever families
